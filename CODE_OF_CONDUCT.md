# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

HamTab is a community project for amateur radio operators. We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Being respectful and considerate in communication
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory remarks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, the Discord server, and any other community channel.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at the email address listed in the repository. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.

Project maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct may face consequences as determined by other members of the project leadership.
