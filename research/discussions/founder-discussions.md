# Founder Discussions

Shared discussion board for founder questions, feedback, and answers.
Full protocol: `ai-workflows/reference/founder-discussions.md`.

**Statuses:** `open` | `answered` | `needs-research` | `needs-discussion` | `decision-needed`

**Rules:**
- Any founder can post questions or feedback
- Items are archived ONLY after all active founders have reviewed
- `needs-research` → delegated via research protocol
- `decision-needed` → creates a DEC entry for founder vote
